Prompt Relay

A broken-telephone party game. Only Player 1 sees the target image and describes it. Each next player sees only the picture the player before them made — never the target — and describes that, so the AI redraws it and the image drifts down the chain. The AI judge scores your final image against the target's secret details.

How it plays

  1. Step 1: only Player 1 sees the target. They have 30 seconds to describe it → the AI draws the base image.
  2. Steps 2 & 3: the next player sees only the previous player's image (not the target), describes what they see → the AI redraws it from scratch.
  3. No talking or coaching — the picture is the only thing passed on. That's what makes it broken telephone.
  4. Timer hits 0 → whatever is typed is auto-submitted. Empty box = a forfeited turn.
  5. Reveal: the target, your relay, and your percentage score.
