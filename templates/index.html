Prompt Relay

A prompt-telephone party game. Your group has 3 timed turns to recreate a hidden reference image — but only Player 1 ever sees it. Every 30-second prompt edits the picture on screen. The AI judge scores your final image against its 10 secret details.

How it plays

  1. Player 1 sees the reference for 30 seconds, then it hides. They may coach the group out loud — but never show it.
  2. Step 1: Player 1 types a prompt → the AI generates the base image.
  3. Steps 2 & 3: each next player edits the current image with a new prompt.
  4. Timer hits 0 → whatever is typed is auto-submitted. Empty box = a forfeited turn.
  5. Reveal: the reference, its 10 details, your relay, and your score out of 10.
